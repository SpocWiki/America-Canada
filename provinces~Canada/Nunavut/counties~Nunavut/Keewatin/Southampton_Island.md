## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Southampton Island (Inuktitut: Shugliaq) 
> is a large island at the entrance to Hudson Bay at Foxe Basin. 
> 
> One of the larger members of the Arctic Archipelago, 
> Southampton Island is part of the Kivalliq Region in Nunavut, Canada. 
> 
> The area of the island is stated as 41,214 km2 (15,913 sq mi) by Statistics Canada. 
> It is the 34th largest island in the world and Canada's ninth largest island. 
> The only settlement on Southampton Island is Coral Harbour 
> (population 1,035, 2021 Canadian census), called Salliq in Inuktitut.
>
> Southampton Island is one of the few Canadian areas, and the only area in Nunavut, 
> that does not use daylight saving time.
>
