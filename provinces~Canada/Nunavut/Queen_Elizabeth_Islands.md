## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> The Queen Elizabeth Islands (French: Îles de la Reine-Élisabeth) 
> are the northernmost cluster of islands in Canada's Arctic Archipelago, 
> split between Nunavut and the Northwest Territories in Northern Canada. 
> 
> The Queen Elizabeth Islands contain approximately 14% of the global glacier 
> and ice cap area (excluding the inland and shelf ice sheets of Greenland and Antarctica). 
> 
> The southern islands are called the Parry Islands or Parry Archipelago.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Queen%20Elizabeth%20Islands)
